# Meta & Google Ads Strategy

This document outlines my approach to building and scaling profitable ad campaigns
using Meta Ads and Google Ads for eCommerce brands.

## Meta Ads Strategy
- Audience research and buyer intent targeting
- Funnel-based campaign structure (TOF, MOF, BOF)
- Creative testing and scaling winning ads
- Retargeting strategies to increase ROAS
- Continuous performance optimization

## Google Ads Strategy
- High-intent keyword research
- Search campaigns for buyer-ready traffic
- Google Shopping setup and optimization
- Conversion tracking and ROAS optimization
- Budget scaling based on performance

## Goal
Drive consistent, scalable sales while maintaining profitability.
